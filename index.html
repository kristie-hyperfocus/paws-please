About Paws Please

A groomer who genuinely loves your dog.

Paws Please is Leanne's home-based grooming salon in Moorooka, built on the belief that every dog deserves to be treated like family. Using only sustainable, eco-friendly products and taking the time to understand each dog's individual needs.

🌿
Fully sustainable

Every product is eco-friendly and gentle — especially important for dogs with sensitive skin.

🏠
Home salon, low stress

A calm, quiet environment — not a chaotic commercial salon. Your dog gets Leanne's full attention.

😌
Brilliant with nervous dogs

Leanne's patient, calm approach has won over even the most anxious pups. Their comfort always comes first.

🐕
Individual care, every time

Leanne takes the time to understand your dog's specific needs — no cookie-cutter grooms here.
